## Visual Language Summary

**Signature motif**: The glass/lens held over the eye. B&W photo, color revealed through the glass. Represents: clarity, seeing what others miss, cutting through noise.

**Prism/refraction imagery**: Crystal prisms, glass cubes, light beams splitting into spectrums. Represents: the Brand Therapy process (raw input enters, focused clarity exits).

**Monolith/slab imagery**: Dark slabs with light breaking through. Represents: transformation, the gap between confusion and clarity.

**Waveform**: Chaotic lines becoming clean signal. Represents: noise-to-signal, the core value proposition.

**Color system**: Matte Black (#0A0A0A) as premium base, Brand Purple (#5A5CF9) as accent/statement, Off-White (#FAFAF8) as breathing space. Purple bottom edge (business card callback) as subtle signature.

**Texture reference**: Matte black business card = the tactile feeling the digital brand should evoke. Grain/matte texture on dark surfaces only.

**Photography style**: B&W with selective color reveals. Editorial but warm. Professional but not corporate.
