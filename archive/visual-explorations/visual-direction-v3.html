Design Principles

What Makes This Bolder.

01

Photography at Scale

No more small avatars or cropped thumbnails. Your photos fill sections. The lens portrait IS the hero, not a sidebar.

02

Dark as Punctuation

1-2 dark sections per page max. The Process section and CTA go dark with grain texture and glass cards. Everything else breathes in light.

03

The Purple Edge

A 3px purple line at the bottom of every dark section. It's your business card. It's subtle. It accumulates into recognition.

04

Prism as Throughline

The prism/lens concept shows up in imagery, in the animated cover element, in the way sections transition from dark to light. Not forced. Woven in.

05

Scroll-Triggered Reveals

Content fades up as you scroll. Stats count up when visible. Marquee tagline scrolls. Subtle motion that rewards attention.

06

White Space as Design

Empty is elegant. Generous padding. Sections breathe. Nothing is packed. The brand's confidence shows in what it doesn't fill.
