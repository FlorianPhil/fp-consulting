Brand Therapy

A diagnostic-first clarity process. Not a rebrand. Not a content plan. A structured conversation that gets to the root of what makes you... you.

Built around the Focus Star framework, it maps five dimensions to find the position that's already yours. You just haven't said it out loud yet.

Audience
Not everyone. The specific people who need what only you offer.
Purpose
Why you do this work. What drives you beneath the title.
DNA
Your non-negotiables. How you operate and what you refuse to compromise.
Personality
How you sound and show up. Your archetype and communication style.
Offering
The category you own, the problem you solve, the transformation you deliver.
