Where do you sit?

Every brand lives somewhere on the spectrum.

From faceless companies to personal brands... the ones that win know exactly where they stand. And they own it.

Company Brand The Sweet Spot Personal Brand

The invisible brand.

Great product, zero personality. Competes on features and price because nobody knows who's behind it. Interchangeable.

The emerging brand.

The company has a name, maybe a decent logo. But the founder is the real magnet. The brand hasn't caught up yet.

This is where leverage lives.

The founder's credibility amplifies the company. The company's structure scales the founder. They feed each other. This is where Brand Therapy takes you.

The founder IS the brand.

Massive personal presence, but the business can't exist without them. Every deal, every post, every decision runs through one person.

Pure personal brand.
