## Brand Therapy — What It Is
Most brand projects start with deliverables. Brand Therapy starts with the truth.

It's a diagnostic-first approach to brand strategy... for founders, executives, and leadership teams who want clarity that drives decisions, not just a better-looking website.

The conversation isn't a formality. It's where the strategy begins.

Built around the Focus Star framework, it maps five dimensions to find the position that's already yours. You just haven't said it out loud yet.

### Focus Star Dimensions
1. **Audience** — Not everyone. The specific people who need what only you offer.
2. **Purpose** — Why you do this work. What drives you beneath the title.
3. **DNA** — Your non-negotiables. How you operate and what you refuse to compromise.
4. **Personality** — How you sound and show up. Your archetype and communication style.
5. **Offering** — The category you own, the problem you solve, the transformation you deliver.

## Process — Four Steps to Clarity
01. **Discovery Call** — In 20 min we find out if we're a fit, what success actually looks like for you, and whether now is the right time to move.
02. **Brand Therapy Blueprint** — A focused strategy session using the Focus Star to uncover your real positioning, your differentiators, and what makes you the obvious choice in your market.
03. **Authority Takeover** — Strategy becomes substance. Messaging, visual identity, professional positioning, and the copy that makes the right people reach out.
04. **Show Up Like You Mean It** — Your brand is ready. Now it goes to work. Content direction, messaging clarity, and a clear lane for showing up with the kind of consistency that builds a reputation.
