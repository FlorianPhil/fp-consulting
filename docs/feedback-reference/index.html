Editorial Presence + Clarity Lens / Creative Direction

The Vision (Summary)

Core concept: "Clarity as a visual experience." The visual language should feel like the Brand Therapy process itself: things coming into focus, being uncovered, seen through a lens for the first time.

Magazine cover metaphor: The brand presents Florian the way a magazine presents its cover subject. The subconscious message: Brand Therapy helps brands become "front page worthy." Big title, subject cutout, metadata at corners like a masthead.

Dark-to-light narrative: Homepage starts dark (confusion) and transitions to light (clarity). This IS the Brand Therapy promise. Not a dark website. A clarity journey. Transition happens fast.

Glass/lens metaphor: Not a trend. Connects to the lens photo, the prism concept, the idea of seeing clearly for the first time. Shows up as text being "uncovered" on scroll, subtle glass panels, the feeling that clarity is being applied in real time.

Purple as ultraviolet: Part of the light spectrum. Not just a brand color but part of the visual story about light and seeing.

The goal: "I want people to share my website just because of how cool it looks. Not even because of the content, but because of how impressive it is."
